In-Person Training

It’s never been easier to get quick and affordable access to the latest professional learning and development to strengthen your practice and behaviour support strategies.

More than twenty experts have partnered with us to provide you with the skills, tools and knowledge you need online or face-to-face.

Our in-person group training includes this and more:

  • Protection and Safeguarding Children
  • Child Protection - an introduction or refresher course
  • Staff Selection – safer recruitment strategies for success
  • Responding to an Allegation of Abuse Against Staff
  • Keeping Children and Young People Safe Online
  • Behaviour Guidance
  • Assisting Behaviour Change in Children
  • Developing a Positive Behaviour Support Plan
  • Strategies for Challenging Behaviour with our Rangatahi 10+ Years
  • Working with Children with ADHD
  • ADHD in Teens
  • Working with Children with Autism
  • Health and Safety
  • Health & Safety - safer sites safer children
  • Health & Safety - safer excursions safer children
  • Child First Aid
  • Food Control Plan Training for the Free and Healthy School Lunches Programme
  • Safe Food Handler and Food Control Plan Training for Staff
  • Nutrition Education
  • Pool Safety Awareness
  • Open Water Safety Awareness
  • Inclusion Training in a Sport or Recreation Setting
  • Developing your Emergency Response Plan - a course for after school care providers
  • Cultural Competency
  • Cultural Awareness and the Treaty of Waitangi
  • Te Reo for Beginners
  • Other Training
  • Creating a Positive Workplace Culture
  • Self-care and Staff Well-being
  • Increasing Engagement with Staff & Tricky Parents

If you don't see exactly what you need in the list above, let us know and we will put together customised training for you.

If you would like to discuss in-person training options for your school, organisation, or professional body contact Safe for Children today.
