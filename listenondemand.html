Listen On Demand

All our live online trainings are recorded, and most can be purchased as a listen On-Demand recording for you to watch at a time that is convenient for you.

Choose the listen On-Demand recording that is right for you and start applying the knowledge today.

Our recordings are in MP4 format and can be played on computers, laptops, Macs, tablets, smartphones, and other mobile devices.

To achieve your certificate of training, view the recording, successfully complete the accompanying quiz and your certificate will be emailed for download.

Safe for Children account holders have access to our full library of listen On-Demand recordings.

To become a Safe for Children account holder please contact us on 0800 7233454 (0800 SAFE4KIDS) or email ✉ admin@safeforchildren.co.nz to request an account holder application form.
