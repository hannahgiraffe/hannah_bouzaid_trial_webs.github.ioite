Live Online Training

Learn from a team of more than twenty expert partners who will provide you with the latest information on child protection, behaviour guidance, health and safety, cultural competency, and self-care. Within our extensive and certified training programme, you will find professional learning and development solutions that fit your needs.

Our convenient, comprehensive, certificated live online training focuses on providing you with relevant ‘apply today and must know’ information and techniques for equipping you to be your best for our children!

Log in and listen at home or work – even on your mobile device!

To gain your Safe for Children certificate of training, we invite you to complete a series of multi choice questions.

Can’t make the live online training date?
No problem – our trainings are recorded so if you miss the live date you will automatically be sent a recording the next day.
Contact us today to find out how you can register to view a live recording.
