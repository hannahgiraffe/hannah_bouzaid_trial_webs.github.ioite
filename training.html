In-Person Training

Certified strength-based training for childcare, education and recreation professionals on child protection, behaviour guidance, health and safety, cultural competency, staff wellbeing and more. Our in-person training can be face to face or live and online.


With more than twenty expert partners already on board, it has never been easier to quickly and effectively manage your professional learning and development needs - live and online.

Our comprehensive certificated training focuses on strengthening your practice with up to date information, strategies, and tools for equipping you to be your best for our children.

Courses available cover all your child protection, behaviour guidance, health and safety, cultural competency, and self-care training needs.

Join on the go? Listen on the go? Choose the training that works for you!
